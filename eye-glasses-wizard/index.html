At Corben Optometry, all three are equally important. Caring, compassionate doctors of optometry, the largest selection of designer eyewear and budget glasses to suit all of you and your family's needs right here in the Santa Clarita Valley. Our contact lens selection is second to none in the valley. Most of our patients leave the very same day seeing comfortably and well even if they have never worn contact lenses before. With an on-site lab, we can make eyeglasses in an hour for most eyeglass prescriptions. We are also board certified to treat eye infections, red eyes, and remove foreign bodies from eyes. Corben Optometry has been providing eye care to the Santa Clarita Valley for 2 generations.

Dr. Michael Corben has been practicing Optometry in Santa Clarita for over 30 years. He is a graduate of Southern California School of Optometry. He has received many distinguished awards for Optometry and has been active in many local community organizations.

Joshua joined the practice in 2002. He graduated from Arizona State University with a B.S. in Biology. While at ASU, he did extensive research in the area of Cell Biology specializing in Human Corneal wound healing. He attained his Doctor of Optometry from The inter-American School of Optometry in 2002. Aside from practicing Optometry, he is a guest lecturer at the nursing school at the College of the Canyons, volunteers for the Red Cross and is heavily involved within the community. He is currently serving as the past president of the San Fernando Valley Optometric Society. Dr. Josh just received the prestigious "Young Optometrist of The Year award", from the California Optometric Association.
